Welcome to The Code Creation

The Code Creation is a non profit organisation powered by Khabai Tech. It's initiative to promote teens for the exposure of digital transformation. As in this 21st era technology has become a critical layer of all our lives and only coding can bring us closer to it. The Code Creation empower teens to enhance their creativity through coding since they really merit an opportunity to be creators rather than consumers. We aim to design the future gems to be eligible to work with technologies around them. Just they required the eagerness to expose themselves.

MISSION & VISION

“Our Mission is to promote students towards digital transformation.”
“Our Vision is - We want to transform students totally digital with realtime technologies.”
“Our Motto is #PromoteStudentsDigitally”

WHAT DO WE DO?

“We used to promote students in tech community by inspiring young students to learn programming.
Through our platform and intuitive curriculum, we plan to give rise to a new generation of young programmers.”
